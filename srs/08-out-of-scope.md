# SRS 08 — Out of Scope — ITER MVP

> Features explicitly excluded from MVP to prevent scope creep.

## Excluded Features (Per Product Brief §9)

### AI Features
- AI travel planner
- AI trip summarization
- AI recommendations
- Voice AI interface

### Booking
- Flight search
- Hotel booking
- Reservation management
- Payment processing

### Advanced Stickers
- Rare stickers (hidden spots)
- Secret stickers (partnerships)
- Sticker trading

### Social Features
- Comments
- Traveler Connect (ID-verified meetups)
- Direct messaging
- Shared trip albums
- Follower management (basic follow/unfollow only in MVP)

### Content
- Photo book printing
- Physical product integration
- User-generated content moderation (basic report/block only)

### Platform
- Native iOS/Android (MVP is Flutter)
- B2B data dashboards
- AI hardware integration

## Rationale

These features require:
- Additional infrastructure (payments, AI, moderation)
- Higher development cost
- More complex UX flows
- Business development (partnerships, B2B)

MVP validates the core hypothesis:
> "Do users want a map-first travel record app?"

## Post-MVP Consideration

If MVP succeeds, prioritize by:
1. User demand (feedback, metrics)
2. Business value (revenue, engagement)
3. Technical feasibility (team capacity, dependencies)
